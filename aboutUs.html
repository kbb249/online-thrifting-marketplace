About LinkU

Our Mission

LinkU was created to give NAU students a safe, organized, and reliable way to buy and sell second-hand items on campus — without the danger, chaos, or inconsistency of informal Snapchat transactions.

At NAU, students commonly rely on Snapchat and other social media platforms to buy and sell items. These transactions often lack structure, safety, and accountability. Our team sought to address this issue by creating a platform designed specifically for students, requiring verification and encouraging pickups in monitored, high-traffic campus areas.

LinkU provides a clean, intuitive interface where students can list items, browse products, and coordinate meetups — all while avoiding the hassles and risks of traditional marketplace platforms.
